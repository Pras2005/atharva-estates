OUR LEGACY-
A FOUNDATION OF TRUST

The journey began in 1989, when Milind Shirude set out with a vision to uplift lifestyles in Nashik by creating real value through quality construction. What started with a foundation of hard work and an uncompromising approach to excellence eventually took formal shape as Atharva Enterprises in 2003. Over the decades, this journey has evolved into more than just building structures; it has been about understanding how people live and what truly makes a space feel complete. Every project is designed with a singular intent: making life simpler, more comfortable, and more meaningful.

Strategic Presence: Nashik & Pune

Our growth is defined by our adaptability. With a strong operational presence in both Nashik and Pune, Atharva Enterprises has successfully navigated the unique demands of two distinct real estate markets. From the fast-paced, high-density requirements of Pune to the evolving urban landscape of Nashik, our ability to execute diverse projects across multiple cities demonstrates our versatility and professional reach. This multi-market experience allows us to bring "big city" innovation and localized expertise to every development we undertake.

Evolution into Hospitality

In 2018, guided by this same forward-thinking vision, Mr. Shirude recognized an evolving need within Nashik for elevated hospitality and premium event spaces. This led to a strategic partnership with Enrise by Sayaji, a renowned 3-star hotel brand. By bringing structured, reliable, and high-quality hospitality to the city, Atharva Enterprises expanded its reach, offering guests a seamless blend of comfort and thoughtful service tailored for both business travellers and leisure seekers.

The Atharva Promise

Built on a foundation of trust and strengthened by lasting relationships, Atharva Enterprises continues to evolve alongside the community it serves. Today, with over 35 years of collective experience-rooted in the early ventures shared by Mr. Shirude and his brothers-our foundation remains unchanged: built with purpose, strengthened by experience, and dedicated to the future of Maharashtra's urban landscape.
